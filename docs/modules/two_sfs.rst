TwoSFS, TwoLocusSFS and TwoSpectra classes
------------------------------------------

The two-dimensional (two-site) site-frequency spectrum: a square matrix relating derived-allele
counts at pairs of frequency classes, together with its two-locus specialization and the collection
type returned when the two-site SFS is parsed with stratifications (one spectrum per within-stratum type).
